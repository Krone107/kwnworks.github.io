The Chick with Courage

Year

Designed for young children around 5-8 years old, "The Chick With Courage" is a pop-up carousel storybook with an original story about how having a little bit of courage can help one make a new friend, and that some people may not be as scary as they look!

This story is inspired from my younger years, often hesitating to approach some people due to how intimidating they look. As I grew older, I learned to get rid of that perception. Through this story, I hope to encourage others to step out of their comfort zones too.
