Modernitea

Category

Typeface Design

Year

2025

Year

2025

Modernitea is a humanist sans-serif typeface designed to express the tradition and culture of Chinese tea in a modern way. Inspired by tea leaves and teaware, Modernitea’s softer edges and variation in stroke thickness give it an elegant and calligraphic look, reflecting the sophisticated practices and values in Chinese tea culture. Combined with more simplistic stems, the balance between modernity and tradition invites viewers to appreciate the richness Chinese tea offers even till today.
