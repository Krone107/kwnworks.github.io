Title

Did You Know - The Calendar Theory

Category

Motion Graphics

Year

2025

Year

2025

"Did you know '_____'" is an assignment tasked to create a short explainer video about any topic we found interesting. As I recently learnt about a theory tying the number of playing cards in a deck to time before the project, I thought it would be fun to show this visually as I reckon it is a topic that has never come across the average person's mind.

Being more of a spiritual topic due to the relations to time, I chose to give a more mysterious and minimalistic take for the video, using flat colours and thinner lines accompanied with atmospheric background sound effects and textures adding a slight retro mood. The smooth, seamless animation and transitions brings the viewer through a visually engaging journey, effectively connecting math and time.
